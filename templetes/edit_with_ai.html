{% if product.SKU %}Editing a Product{% else %}Adding a New Product{% endif %}

This form lets you {% if product.SKU %}update an existing product's details{% else %}create a brand-new product in your inventory{% endif %}. Fill in the fields and save when done.

💡 Use the AI Photo Analysis section at the top to auto-fill most fields by just uploading a photo!
🤖
Step 1 — AI Analysis

Let AI Fill In the Details

  1. Click "Choose File" in the AI Photo Analysis box.
  2. Select a clear photo of your item.
  3. Click "✨ Analyze Photo".
  4. Claude AI will automatically fill in the title, description, category, condition, and a suggested price.
  5. Review the results and adjust anything before saving.
🏷️
Step 2 — Basic Info

Basic Information Fields

  • SKU — unique ID for the item (e.g. ITEM-001). Required. Cannot be changed after saving.
  • Title — the product name shown on all pages and listings.
  • Description — details about the item, its condition, history, etc.
SKUs must be unique. If you leave it blank, one will not be auto-generated — you must enter one.
💰
Step 3 — Details & Pricing

Details & Pricing Fields

  • Category — used for filtering and seasonal sales (e.g. "Furniture", "Clothing").
  • Price — the sale price shown to customers.
  • Cost Paid — what you paid for it (admin-only, used for profit tracking).
  • Condition — e.g. Excellent, Good, Fair.
  • Status — Available or Sold.
📍
Step 4 — Location & Images

Location & Product Images

  • Section — the area of the store where this item lives (e.g. "Back Wall").
  • Shelf — more specific location within the section (e.g. "Shelf 3").
  • Images — upload one or more photos. The first photo is used as the thumbnail on the dashboard.
Location fields help staff find items in the store quickly.
💾
Step 5 — Save

Saving Your Product

  1. Review all fields to make sure everything looks correct.
  2. Click the "Save Product" button at the bottom of the form.
  3. You'll be taken back to the Dashboard where the product will appear in the grid.
A backup of your inventory is automatically created every time you save a product.
