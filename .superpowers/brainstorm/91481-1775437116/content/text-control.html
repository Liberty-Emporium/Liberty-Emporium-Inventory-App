How much control do you want over the text?

Claude will suggest the words — but how much can you edit before generating?

A

Full Auto

Claude picks everything — headline, price line, tagline, style. You click Generate and get the finished ad. No editing unless you regenerate.

B

Review & Edit Before Generating

Claude drafts the headline, tagline, and style recommendation. You see editable text boxes and can tweak anything before the image is rendered. Like a "preview draft" step.

C

Edit on the Generated Image

Claude generates the image. You can then click on text areas in the result and retype them, then re-render. Edit after seeing it.
