Managing Users

Learn how to review account requests, approve or reject new users, and manage who has access to your store's inventory system.

🔒 Only the Owner (admin) account can access User Management.
🗺️
Step 1 of 6 — Accessing the Page

How to Open User Management

There are two ways to reach the User Management page:

📊

Via the Dashboard

Scroll to the Admin Control Panel section and click 👥 Manage Users.

⚠️

Via an Alert Banner

When new users are waiting, a yellow banner appears at the top of the dashboard. Click "Review Now".

🔗

Direct URL

Navigate directly to /admin/users while logged in as admin.

You must be logged in as admin to access this page. Other users will be redirected to the dashboard.
🖥️
Step 2 of 6 — Page Overview

What You'll See

The page is divided into two clear sections:

⏳

Pending Approval

Users who signed up and are waiting for you to grant or deny access. Shows username, email, and the date they requested access.

✅

Approved Users

Users who already have access. The Owner (admin) always appears here. Other approved staff members are listed below.

The number in parentheses — e.g. Pending Approval (2) — tells you exactly how many items are in each section at a glance.
✅

Step 4 of 6 — Rejecting a User

How to Reject a Request

Not every signup request should be approved. Rejecting removes it permanently.

⏳ Pending Approval (1)

unknown_person · Requested: 2026-03-29
  • 1
    Find the unwanted request in Pending Approval.
  • 2
    Click the red Reject button next to their name.
  • 3
    A confirmation dialog will appear — click OK to confirm.
  • 4
    The request is deleted and the person cannot log in.
⚠️ Rejection is permanent. The person would need to sign up again if you change your mind.

Step 6 of 6 — Dashboard Notification

Stay Notified on the Dashboard

You don't have to check the Users page manually. The dashboard automatically alerts you when requests are waiting.

⏳ 2 account request(s) waiting for your approval.
📊

Auto Alert

A yellow banner appears on the dashboard whenever there are pending signup requests.

🖱️

One Click Away

Click the "Review Now" button inside the alert to jump straight to User Management.

The alert disappears automatically once all pending requests have been approved or rejected.
