Picture Ad Studio — How-To Guide

Generate Beautiful JPEG Ads

The Picture Ad Studio creates polished 1080px JPEG images for your products — ready to post on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, or print in-store. Generate up to 10 ads at once.

☑️
Step 1 — Select Products

Choose Your Products

  1. Browse the product grid below.
  2. Click any card to select it — a checkmark and gold border appear.
  3. Click again to deselect. You can select up to 10 products at once.
Only Available products are shown. Sold items are excluded.
🎨
Step 2 — Pick a Style

Choose Your Look

  • Elegant — Deep navy with gold accents
  • Vivid — Bold red with yellow pop
  • Forest — Rich green, earthy feel
  • Ocean — Deep blue, cool tones
  • Warm — Amber espresso, cosy vibe
⚡
Step 4 — Generate

Create Your Ads

  1. Optionally add your Store Name and a CTA line.
  2. Click "✨ Generate Ads" in the sticky bar at the bottom.
  3. Ads appear as full-size previews below — click Download to save each one.
Generation takes just a few seconds per product.
