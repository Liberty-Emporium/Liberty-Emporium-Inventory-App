Ad Generator — How-To Guide

Generate Ready-to-Post Ads

The Ad Generator uses AI to create polished JPEG advertisement images for your products — perfect for Facebook, Instagram, or printing in-store. You can generate ads for up to 10 products at once.

☑️
Step 1 — Select Products

Choosing Your Products

  1. Browse the product grid below the style picker.
  2. Click any product card to select it — a checkmark appears and the border highlights.
  3. Click again to deselect it.
  4. The counter in the top-right shows how many are selected (max 10).
Only Available products appear. Sold products are not shown.
🎨
Step 2 — Choose Style

Picking an Ad Style

Before generating, choose a visual style for your ads using the style buttons at the top:

  • Red & Gold — classic, elegant look
  • Navy & Cream — professional and clean
  • Forest & Tan — warm, earthy tone
  • Midnight — bold dark theme
  • Blush — soft, boutique feel
⚡
Step 3 — Generate

Generating Your Ads

  1. With at least one product selected, the "✨ Generate Ads" button becomes active.
  2. Click it — a loading overlay appears while the AI creates your ads.
  3. When done, a list of generated ad files appears below the product grid.
Generation takes a few seconds per product. Don't close the page while it's running.
⬇️
Step 4 — Use Your Ads

Viewing & Downloading Ads

Each generated ad appears as a card in the results section with two buttons:

  • View — opens the ad image in a new browser tab so you can preview it.
  • Download — saves the JPEG file to your computer, ready to post.
Ads are saved on the server. You can re-download them any time by visiting this page again.
🎬
Video Studio

Creating a Video Ad

The purple 🎬 Video Studio panel below the generate bar turns your product photos into MP4 video ads with music. Use the same selected products as image ads.

  1. Pick a Video Style — Slideshow or Ken Burns zoom.
  2. Select a Music Track from the library (click ▶️ to preview) or upload your own MP3.
  3. Set the Duration with the slider (10–60 seconds).
  4. Click "🎬 Create Video Ad" — takes up to a minute.
⬇️
Video Results

Your Generated Videos

Each video appears below the Video Studio panel with an inline preview player and a ⬇️ Download MP4 button. Videos are saved on the server so you can download them any time.

Ken Burns videos may take slightly longer to generate than Slideshow videos due to the zoom animation processing.
