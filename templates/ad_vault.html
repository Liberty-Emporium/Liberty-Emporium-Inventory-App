Downloading Ads

Download an Ad

  1. Find the ad you want in the grid.
  2. Click ⬇️ Download — the JPEG saves to your device.
  3. Click the image itself to open the full-size version in a new tab.
All ads are 1080×1080px — perfect for Instagram, Facebook, and in-store displays.

  2. Confirm the deletion — this cannot be undone.
