Image Editor — How-To Guide

Editing Your Product Photos

The Image Editor lets you rotate and zoom your product photos to get them looking just right before customers see them on the product page and dashboard.

🖱️
Step 1 — Select an Image

Choosing a Photo to Edit

  1. The product's photos appear as small thumbnails at the bottom of the editor.
  2. Click a thumbnail to load it into the main canvas editor above.
  3. The selected image will appear on the canvas, ready to edit.
🔧
Step 2 — Edit Controls

Using the Editor Controls

RotateTurns the image 90° clockwise
Zoom InMakes the image larger on canvas
Zoom OutMakes the image smaller on canvas
ResetUndoes all changes to original
💾
Step 3 — Save

Saving Your Changes

  1. Once the image looks right on the canvas, click the "Save" button.
  2. The edited image is saved and replaces the original photo for this product.
  3. Click the back link to return to the product page and see the updated image.
Each product can have multiple images. Edit and save each one individually using the thumbnails.
