What is this page?

This is your store's main inventory management dashboard. Everything you need to manage products, create listings, and run your resale business is accessible from here.

Core Workflow

  • Add Item — photograph, describe, price, and categorize new inventory
  • Generate Listing — AI writes the sales copy for any item — just pick the platform
  • Track Sales — record when items sell to keep inventory counts accurate
  • Ad Generator — create social media ads for any item or category

Selling More with AI

  • Use Listing Generator to create optimized listings in 30 seconds per item
  • Use Ad Generator to make Facebook/Instagram ad copy for featured items
  • Use Edit with AI to improve descriptions of existing items
Batch your listing creation — add 10 items in a row, then run the listing generator on all of them at once. Much faster than one at a time.
