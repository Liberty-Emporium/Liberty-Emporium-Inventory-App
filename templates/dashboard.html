Dashboard — How-To Guide

Your Inventory Command Center

The Dashboard is the home base of the app. From here you can see all your products, check stats, and access every feature the app offers.

Use the navigation bar at the top to jump to any section of the app.
📈
Stats Grid

Reading Your Stats

The four stat boxes at the top give you an instant snapshot of your inventory:

Total ItemsEverything in inventory
AvailableItems still for sale
SoldItems marked as sold
Total ValueSum of all sale prices
🛍️
Products

Browsing Your Products

Each product card shows the photo, title, SKU, price, and status. Use the buttons on every card to take action:

ViewSee all product details
EditUpdate info or price
Edit ImageRotate or crop photos
Add New ProductTop-right of product list
🔒
Admin Controls

Admin Control Panel

If you're logged in as admin, a row of admin buttons appears above the stats:

  • 👥 Manage Users — approve or remove staff accounts
  • 💾 Backups — save or restore your inventory
  • 📥 Export CSV — download your full inventory
  • ⬛ Export for Square — export in Square's format
  • 📥 Import from Square — bring in Square products
🔥
Seasonal Sale

Sale & Approval Alerts

Two types of banners may appear below the nav bar:

  • 🔥 Sale Active banner — shows when a seasonal discount is running. Click "Manage Sale" to change it.
  • ⏳ Pending Approval banner — alerts you when new users are waiting. Click "Review Now" to go to User Management.
