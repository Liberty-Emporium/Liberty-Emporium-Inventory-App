Seasonal Sale — How-To Guide

Run a Seasonal Sale

The Seasonal Sale page lets you put a category of products on sale at a discounted price and display them as a branded sale event.

🏷️
Step 1 — Set Up the Sale

Configure Your Sale

  1. Go to Seasonal Sale in the admin menu.
  2. Pick the category to put on sale.
  3. Set the discount percentage.
  4. Give it a sale name (e.g. "Summer Clearance").
📢
Step 2 — Share It

Share Your Sale Page

  1. Copy the sale page URL from your browser.
  2. Post it on Facebook, Instagram, or text it to customers.
  3. Customers see a branded page with all discounted items and prices.
The sale page works on mobile — customers can browse and contact you directly from it.
