💡 What's an API key and why do I need one?

Think of an API key like a membership card for an AI service. OpenRouter is the AI service that powers your store's AI assistant. When you create a free account and get a key, it's like handing your store a membership card so it can access the AI. No key = no AI. The key is free to get and free to use for normal store activity.

📋 Step-by-Step: Get Your Free API Key

1
Go to OpenRouter.ai
Open a new tab and visit openrouter.ai — it's free to sign up. Think of it as the AI hub that connects to dozens of AI models including GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini.
2
Create a free account
Click "Sign In" at the top right, then "Create account." You can sign up with Google in one click — no credit card needed.
3
Get your API key
Once logged in, go to openrouter.ai/keys. Click "Create Key," give it any name (like "My Store"), then click "Create." You'll see a long code that starts with sk-or-v1-...
4
Copy it and paste it below
Click the copy button next to your key, come back to this page, paste it into the "OpenRouter API Key" field below, and hit Save. That's it — your AI is ready to go! 🎉
🔒 Keep your key private. Never share it or post it publicly. Treat it like a password. OpenRouter lets you delete or reset it any time at openrouter.ai/keys if you ever need to.
