Adding a New Client

Provision a Store

  1. Click + Add New Client.
  2. Enter the store name, client email, and a temporary password.
  3. Choose a plan and branding color.
  4. Submit — the store is live instantly.
Share the login email and temp password with your client so they can log in.
🔀
Managing a Client Store

Log In as a Client

  1. Click Manage on any client card.
  2. You'll be taken to their dashboard — a yellow banner shows you're in their store.
  3. Add products, generate ads, or do anything they need.
  4. Click Exit to Overseer in the banner to return here.
