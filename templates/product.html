Viewing a Product

This page shows all the details for a single product — photos, price, condition, location, and description. From here you can also share, edit, or delete the item.

📋
Product Details

Reading Product Info

  • SKU — unique identifier for this item.
  • Status badge — green = Available, red = Sold.
  • Price, Condition, Category — key details at a glance.
  • 📍 Location — section and shelf where the item lives in the store.
  • Description — full details about the item.

Sharing

Share This Item

Use the share buttons to promote the item across platforms:

  • 📘 Share on Facebook — opens Facebook with the product link.
  • 📋 Post to Craigslist — opens a pre-filled Craigslist posting.
  • 📸 Copy for Instagram — copies a ready-to-paste caption to your clipboard.
  • 🔗 Copy Link — copies the product URL to share anywhere.
⚙️
Actions

Product Actions

  • Edit Product — update any details, price, or status.
  • Edit Images — rotate or crop the product photos.
  • 🏷️ Print Tag — generate a printable price tag for this item.
  • Delete — permanently removes the product. A confirmation is required.
⚠️ Deleting a product cannot be undone. Use the Backups page if you need to recover data.
